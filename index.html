Work

Founded in 1930, Aigner Chocolates is one of the oldest chocolate and confectionery shops in NYC. Owned by the same family for three generations, the Aigners have recently passed the recipes and traditions of chocolate making to the new owners. Continuing the rich tradition of making Austrian style chocolate using the same antique equipment and techniques that has made Aigner a New York City institution.”

I am very honored to be a part of this local business. Our mission is to spread joy and hope to communities through the magic of our chocolate. We share our passion for the art of chocolate to make the world a better place. At Aigner Chocolates, we hold ourselves to high standards and value the importance of community, teamwork, solution-focused thinking, dedication, and striving for greatness. We believe in working together to create exceptional products that delight our customers while staying dedicated to our craft and continuously improving our processes. Our values guide us in every decision we make, and we strive to build a community that fosters growth and success for all.
